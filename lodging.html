Lodging

Taniti has a wide variety of lodging that ranges from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts. All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Resort

Perched on a lush hillside overlooking the turquoise waters of the Pacific, Azure Cove Resort is Taniti's premier four-star retreat, offering breathtaking views and exclusive private beach access. Wake up to the sound of gentle waves and step onto your private balcony to witness stunning sunrises over the ocean. Azure Cove Resort promises an unforgettable blend of relaxation and adventure in paradise.

Hostel

For travelers on a budget, Driftwood Hostel is the perfect place to stay in Taniti. This charming, laid-back retreat offers unbeatable value without sacrificing comfort. Mingle with fellow explorers, swap travel stories around the nightly beach bonfire, or join group excursions to hike the island's lush rainforest trails. Hotels

Taniti's many small, family-owned hotels offer a warm island welcome with a variety of options to suit every traveler. Wake up to the sound of waves at a charming beachfront inn or immerse yourself in nature at a cozy rainforest retreat nestled in the island's lush interior. Whether you're seeking oceanfront serenity or a quiet escape in the heart of the island, you'll find the perfect home away from home at an unbeatable value!

Bed & Breakfast

For travelers seeking a truly authentic and personalized island experience, Taniti's charming B&Bs have become a favorite choice. With warm hospitality, comfortable accomodations, and delicious homemade breakfasts featuring fresh, local ingredients, these B&Bs offer a welcoming atmosphere and a true taste of island life.
